Leading University Wolfriding since 2024

The Hamilton University Wolfriding team is a college-level wolfriding team based in Washington DC.

Hamilton University is a medium-sized public university of about 20,000 stu- dents. Their Wolfriding team was estab- lished 20 years ago, and grew out of the popular club team at the school. The team has been established for two decades and is a strong competitor in the Eastern Division.
